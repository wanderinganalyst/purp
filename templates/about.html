About BecauseImStuck

Empowering citizens to engage with their representatives and track legislation that matters.

Our Mission

BecauseImStuck is dedicated to making civic engagement accessible, transparent, and meaningful for everyone.

We believe that every citizen should have an easy way to stay informed about legislation, connect with their elected representatives, and make their voices heard in the democratic process.

What We Do

Track Legislation

Stay updated on bills moving through the Missouri legislature with real-time tracking, bill details, and voting records.

Find Your Representatives

Instantly discover who represents you in the State House and Senate based on your address.

Direct Messaging

Communicate directly with your representatives through our secure messaging platform.

Event Engagement

Get invited to town halls, rallies, and community events hosted by your representatives.

Our Values

Transparency

We provide clear, unbiased information about legislation and representatives.

Accessibility

Civic engagement should be easy and available to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Civic Duty

We believe informed citizens are the foundation of a strong democracy.
