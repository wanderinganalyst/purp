How It Works

This AI-powered tool helps you draft new bills by learning from Missouri's legislative history:

  • Passed Bills (✓ Positive Examples): The AI studies successful bills that were signed into law to learn effective language, structure, and approaches.
  • Failed Bills (✗ Negative Examples): The AI analyzes bills that were defeated or rejected to avoid problematic patterns.
  • You'll receive a complete LLM prompt that you can use with ChatGPT, Claude, or other AI tools to generate your bill draft.
